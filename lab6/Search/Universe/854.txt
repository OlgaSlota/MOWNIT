(April 2016) (Learn how and when to remove this template message) Body Of Work Greatest hits album by Nitzer Ebb Released June 5, 2006 (2006-06-05) Recorded 1984-1995 Genre EBM Length 155:00 Label Mute/EMI Records (UK) 0946 3 66247 2 3 MUTEL 9 Mute/Caroline/EMI Records (U.S.) 7245 9 69328 2 7 MUT 69328 Nitzer Ebb chronology Big Hit (1995) Body of Work (2006) Body Rework (2006) Body of Work is a hits collection by UK EBM band Nitzer Ebb.[1] It was released by Mute Records as a double album on June 5, 2006 (2006-06-05). The first disc is the band's single output 1986–1995. The second disc is a collection of remixes from the same period, most of them singles.
