January[edit] Flash Gordon Comic Strip is born on January 7th.[1] July[edit] Famous Funnies #1 (Eastern Color Printing) - The first full-color comic book sold to the public. It set the standard of 68 pages, including covers, and sold for 10 cents. Early issues of this series, starting with #1, advertised the contents as "100 Comics and Games - Puzzles - Magic." July 26: Winsor McCay, creator of Little Nemo, dies at age 64. September[edit] Famous Funnies #2 (Eastern Color Printing) - With this issue, Famous Funnies would become the first monthly newsstand comic publication. October[edit] Famous Funnies #3 (Eastern Color Printing) November[edit] Famous Funnies #4 (Eastern Color Printing) December[edit] Famous Funnies #5 (Eastern Color Printing) First issues by title[edit] Famous Funnies on newsstands in May, cover dated July, published by Eastern Color Printing Company.
