Contents 1 Premise 2 Cast 2.1 Recurring cast 3 Production 4 Broadcast 5 Episodes 5.1 Season 1 (2013–14) 5.2 Season 2 (2014–15) 6 References 7 External links Premise[edit] Danny thinks that Kim might be "the one". His older brothers, who raised him after their parents died, are not so fond of her. Sheldon avoids relationships but is in favor of one-night stands. Ryan was divorced by his wife who hooked up with a fellow surgeon after he put her through medical school. Kim is not happy with how intimately involved Sheldon and Ryan are in Danny's life. Cast[edit] Randal Edwards as Danny, a criminal defense lawyer[1] Harland Williams as Sheldon, a stubborn and politically incorrect salesman[1] Jay Malone as Ryan, a metrosexual former house-husband[1] Julia Voth as Kim Recurring cast[edit] Eugene Levy[2] as McKenzie, a client of Danny's whose wives keep mysteriously dying[1] Pamela Anderson[2] as Dr Sydney Forbes, Ryan's quirky therapist who has an intimate approach[1] Production[edit] Package Deal is a four-camera comedy primarily filmed with a studio audience, which is rare for Canadian television.[3][4] The concept of the show comes from creator Andrew Orenstein's experiences with his family.[4] The first season finale was filmed in Burnaby on 25 January 2013.[3] Package Deal was renewed for a second season on 21 January 2014.[5] Broadcast[edit] On 29 May 2013, Citytv announced that they had commissioned 13 episodes of Package Deal for the 2012-13 mid-season television season.[6] It was announced on 1 April 2013 that the show would premiere on 6 May, taking the spot in the schedule that had been occupied by Seed.[2] Later in April that was changed to 24 June.[1][7] As part of Rogers' upfront presentation on 4 June it was announced that Package Deal had been moved to the autumn and would be on Mondays at 8:30 ET.[8] The series premiere was subsequently again changed to 18 June at 9 p.m. ET/PT, 10 p.m. MT, and 8 p.m. CT and labelled a special preview.[9][10] Episodes[edit] Season Episodes Originally aired First aired Last aired 1 13 18 June 2013 (2013-06-18) 7 January 2014 (2014-01-07) 2 13
