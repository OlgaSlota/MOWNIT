It covers research on the pharmacodynamics and pharmacokinetics of drugs and non-therapeutic xenobiotics. The editor-in-chief is S. J. Enna, University of Kansas Medical Center, Kansas City.[1] Abstracting and indexing[edit] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: BIOSIS Chemical Abstracts Current Contents EMBASE Elsevier BIOBASE MEDLINE PASCAL Scopus According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal received a 2014 impact factor of 5.009, ranking it 23rd out of 254 journals in the category Pharmacology & Pharmacy.[2] References[edit] ^ Website of Professor Enna at Kansas University, accessed on February 11th, 2013 ^ "Journals Ranked by Impact: Pharmacology & Pharmacy". 2014 Journal Citation Reports.
