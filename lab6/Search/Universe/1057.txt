Archaeology (magazine) - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Archaeology (magazine) From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation, search Archaeology March/April 2008 cover of Archaeology Editor in Chief Claudia Valentino Categories Archaeology, science Publisher Peter Herdrich Total circulation (2013) 235,701[1] First issue 1948; 68 years ago (1948) Company Archaeological Institute of America Country USA Language English Website archaeology.org ISSN 0003-8113 Archaeology magazine is a bimonthly mainstream publication for the general public, supported by its website archaeology.org. Profile[edit] The magazine is published by the Archaeological Institute of America. The institute also publishes the professional American Journal of Archaeology.
