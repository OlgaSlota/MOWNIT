January[edit] January 2: Kid Colt artist Jack Keller dies at age 80. March[edit] March 2: Bill Woggon, creator of Katy Keene, dies at age 92. April[edit] Action Comics #800: Double-sized anniversary issue, "A Hero's Journey," by Joe Kelly, Pascual Ferry, and Duncan Rouleau. (DC Comics) April 9: Syndicated cartoonist Jerry Bittle, creator of Geech and Shirley and Son, dies at age 53. April 19: Webcomic Bigtime Consulting comes to a conclusion May[edit] May 27: Al Hartley, long-time Archie Comics and Christian comics artist, dies at age 81. June[edit] Wolverine vol. 2 is canceled by Marvel with issue #189. June 10 - Manhwa Dragon Hunter begins publication[1] July[edit] July 24: Warren Kremer, long-time Harvey Comics creator, passes away at age 82. October[edit] October 12: Pete Morisi dies at age 75. November[edit] November 12: Long-time Marvel Comics inker John Tartaglione dies at age 81. November 30: Kin Platt dies at age 92. December[edit] December 5: Donald Duck comics writer/artist Bob Gregory dies at age 82. December 27: Western Publishing artist Pete Alvarado dies at age 83. December 29: British cartoonist Don Lawrence dies at age 75.
