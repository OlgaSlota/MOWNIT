Flag of Cornwall, Ontario - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Flag of Cornwall, Ontario From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation, search Cornwall Use Design Triband with a Canadian pale The Flag of Cornwall, Ontario features the shield of Arms of the City of Cornwall on a black square (Canadian pale) surmounting a gold field. The shield of Arms of the City of Cornwall's colours are reversed to the arms of the Duchy of Cornwall, the United Kingdom.
