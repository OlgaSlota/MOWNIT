June[edit] June 3: Webcomic A Miracle of Science by Jon Kilgannon and Mark Sachs debuts November[edit] Time Warner was announced to drop the AOL name. Deaths[edit] January[edit] January 4: Kurt Schaffenberger, long-time artist of Captain Marvel and Superman's Girl Friend, Lois Lane, dies at age 81. January 10: Legendary Marvel Comics artist John Buscema passes away at age 74. April[edit] April 22: British cartoonist Denis McLoughlin dies at age 84. May[edit] May 3: Horror comics artist Tom Sutton dies at age 65. May 7: Robert Kanigher, writer of Wonder Woman and most of DC Comics' war comics line, dies at age 86. May 17: Dave Berg, long-time Mad magazine cartoonist, passes away at age 81. June[edit] June 13: Vincent Fago, Golden Age artist and editor, dies at age 83. July[edit] July 8: Ward Kimball, one of Disney's Nine Old Men, dies at age 88 July 12: Argentinian cartoonist Jorge Zaffino dies at c. age 42 August[edit] August 5: Grass Green dies at age 63. September[edit] September 13: Golden Age creator Fred Guardineer passes away at age 88.
