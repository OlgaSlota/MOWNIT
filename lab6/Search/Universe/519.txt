Indexed by ISI Life Sciences received an impact factor of 2.702 as reported in the 2014 Journal Citation Reports by Thomson Reuters, ranking it 53rd out of 123 journals in the category Medicine, research & experimental [1] and ranking it 103rd out of 254 journals in the category "Pharmacology & Pharmacy".[2] The current Editor in Chief is Loren E.
