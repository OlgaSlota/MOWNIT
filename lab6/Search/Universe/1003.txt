K. Laxman, creator of The Common Man, dies at age 93.[8] February[edit] February 16: Brett Ewins, British comic book artist, dies at age 59[9] March[edit] March 13: Irwin Hasen, creator of Dondi, dies at age 96[10] April[edit] April 13: Herb Trimpe, the seminal 1970s artist on The Incredible Hulk, dies at age 75.[11] July[edit] July 20: Tom Moore, long-time artist for Archie, dies at age 86.[12] August[edit] August 30: Brad Anderson, creator of the comic strip Marmaduke, dies at age 91.[13] October[edit] October 8: Dennis Eichhorn, comics writer/editor and creator of Real Stuff, dies of pneumonia at age 70.[14] Conventions[edit] January 29–31: Salt Lake Comic Con FanXperience (Salt Palace Convention Center, Salt Lake City, Utah) — guests include Matt Smith, Karen Gillan, Billie Piper, Carrie Fisher, Christopher Lloyd, Felicia Day, Tom Felton, Steven Yeun, Emily Kinney, Laurie Holden, Lena Headey, Nikolaj Coster-Waldau, Brandon Routh, Ray Park, Paul Wesley, Phoebe Tonkin, Nichelle Nichols, Anthony Michael Hall, Mark Pellegrino, Alaina Huffman, Ralph Macchio, Glenn Morshower, RJ Mitte, Jim Cummings, Jess Harnell, Rob Paulsen and over 100 other guests[15] January 29 – February 1: Angoulême International Comics Festival (Angoulême, France) March 7: New York Comic Book Marketplace (Hotel New Yorker, New York City) — guests include Jason David Frank, "Rowdy" Roddy Piper, Jake "The Snake" Roberts, Rich Buckler, Bill Plympton, Bob Camp, and Johnny Brennan March 7–8: STAPLE!
