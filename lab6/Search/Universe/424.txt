Former church building. Saint Rose of Lima Catholic Church in Gaithersburg is a parish of the Roman Catholic Church of Maryland in the United States. It falls under the jurisdiction of the Archdiocese of Washington and its archbishop. It is named after Saint Rose of Lima of Peru. Mass is held in both English and Spanish.
