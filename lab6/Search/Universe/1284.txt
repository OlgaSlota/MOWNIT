Pol-ka - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Pol-ka From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation, search Pol-ka Producciones is a television and film production company in Buenos Aires, Argentina, commonly referred to as Pol-ka. The company was launched in 1994 and is owned by Adrián Suar, Fernando Blanco and Artear. The firm was started when their first television show was produced: "Poliladron" (1994). After producing a series of hits for Canal 13, Adrián Suar became the programming director of the channel. The production company is responsible for the fictions the channel air in primetime. Pol-ka is the producing company in Argentina with the highest-number of awards.
