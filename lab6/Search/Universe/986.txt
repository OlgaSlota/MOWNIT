February[edit] February 3: Pioneering editor Vin Sullivan dies at age 87.[1] February 26: John L. Goldwater, co-founder of Archie Comics, dies at age 82.[2] March[edit] The Incredible Hulk (1968 series) is canceled by Marvel with issue #474. May[edit] Anarky, vol. 2 - DC Comics May 30: Prolific scripter Paul S. Newman, creator of Turok, dies at age 75. July[edit] July 1: James Sanchez begins publishing the webcomic Bigtime Consulting. July 16: Words & Pictures Museum of Fine Sequential Art closes its doors to the public, becoming the Virtual Words & Pictures Museum.[3] August[edit] 100 Bullets, by Brian Azzarello and Eduardo Risso, debuts under the Vertigo imprint (cover date). November[edit] Strange Adventures vol. 2, #1 - Vertigo Deaths[edit] March[edit] March 8': Giovan Battista Carpi, comics artist for Disney comics, dies at age 71. March 14: John Broome, comic book writer for DC Comics, dies at age 85. April[edit] April 12: Ricardo Barreiro, comic book writer, dies at age 49. May[edit] May 12: Saul Steinberg, cartoonist, dies at age 84. June[edit] June 15: John Glashan, creator of Genius, dies at age 71. August[edit] August 8: Yolanda Vargas Dulché, creator of Memín Pinguín, died at age 73. August 13: John Geering, creator of Puss 'n' Boots, Smudge and Bananaman, dies at age 58. August 30: Raymond Poïvet, creator of Les Pionniers de l'Espérance, at age 89. October[edit] October 29: Greg, creator of Achille Talon, dies at age 68.
