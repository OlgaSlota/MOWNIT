Publisher Blatant Comics founded by Chris Crosby and Bobby Crosby. WildStorm established the Cliffhanger imprint. April[edit] Comic strip Cigarman begins publication in Smoke Magazine Marvel cancels The Punisher (1995 series) with issue #18 July[edit] July was Marvel Comics' Flashback month. All issues were released with the number −1. November[edit] Mayo Kaan takes out newspaper ads claiming to be the original model for Superman,[1] although his allegations were later proven false.[2] December[edit] Marvel returns the Avengers and Fantastic Four to their main continuity in Heroes Reborn: The Return #1–4 Deaths[edit] Tony Velasquez, the Father of Tagalog comics, dies at age 86. January[edit] January 25: Dan Barry dies at age 73. February[edit] February 15: Jack Sparling dies at age 81. March[edit] March 10: Stan Drake dies at age 75. June[edit] Ruth Atkinson, co-creator of Patsy Walker and Millie the Model, passes away at age 78. November[edit] December 13: Samm Schwartz, long-time artist of Jughead stories for Archie Comics, passes away at age 75. Exhibitions and shows[edit] This section is empty.
