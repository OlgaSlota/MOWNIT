Attributive - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Attributive From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation, search Look up attributive in Wiktionary, the free dictionary. In grammar, an attributive is a word or phrase within a noun phrase that modifies the head noun. It may be an: attributive adjective attributive noun attributive verb or other part of speech, such as an attributive numeral.
