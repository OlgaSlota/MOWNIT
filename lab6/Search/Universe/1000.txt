Deaths[edit] February[edit] February 29: Artist Sheldon Moldoff dies at age 91. March[edit] March 10: Artist Jean Giraud (Mœbius) dies at age 73 from cancer.[1][2] May[edit] May 11: Artist Tony DeZuniga dies at age 79 following a stroke. May 16: Artist Ernie Chan dies at age 71 of cancer. August[edit] August 12: Joe Kubert, known for his work on Sgt. Rock, Hawkman, and Tarzan, and founder of The Kubert School, dies at age 85. November[edit] November 13: Ray Zone, American film historian, author and illustrator, 65 November 30: Tank McNamara writer Jeff Millar dies of bile duct cancer at age 70.
